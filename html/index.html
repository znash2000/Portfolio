Portfolio of Zoë Nash

I am Zoë Nash and, in this portfolio, I will be telling you guys a bit about me and my skills and capabilities, as well as showcase some of my work.

I am a simple girl from Cape Town, South Africa and I love designing and showing my creative side. I express my creativity through virtual art like logo’s, icons and digital marketing content. Growing up, I always knew that I wanted to work in the creative and design field, hence choosing Multimedia as my major. With studying Multimedia, I learnt about high tech software’s like the Adobe design tools and also about branding. I got exposed to many different design styles and managed to figure out my own design style. I worked with colour palettes and got to explore the whole process of creating and customizing colours. I was given more insight into branding when we started learning about brand compliance and sticking to a brand type. Basically, we were given a company’s theme, brand guidelines and colours and we had to design around those rules. This helped me a lot because it taught me to stick to what the client wants and to respect their wishes and just implement my skills.

I enjoy creating digital content because I get to show my type of design style which is precise, yet minimal. Designing content has no limitations so there are no rights or wrong, it is all about what is needed and required by the client, keeping in mind how it will be viewed and how the message will be conveyed. Creating digital content is not just making something for a client, it is about providing a service that meets the needs of a company or client. Whether it is designing a simple letterhead or basic business card, I always enjoy it because I find designing and creating things fun.

Hopefully, by exploring my portfolio, you will see my capabilities and design abilities! Enjoy!
